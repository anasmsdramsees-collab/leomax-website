# Best Business Podcasts

A curated list of useful business podcasts for learning entrepreneurship, strategy, branding, scaling, investment thinking, and digital transformation.

## Top Recommendations

### 1. Founders
Best for understanding the mindset of great founders and business builders such as Steve Jobs, Jeff Bezos, Walt Disney, and Rockefeller.

Link: https://www.founderspodcast.com/

### 2. Acquired
Best for learning how major companies were built, how they scaled, how they make money, and how strategic decisions shaped their growth.

Link: https://www.acquired.fm/

### 3. My First Million
Best for practical business ideas, market opportunities, trends, and conversations around building profitable ventures.

Link: https://www.mfmpod.com/

### 4. How I Built This
Best for founder stories, brand-building journeys, and understanding how entrepreneurs turned ideas into companies.

Link: https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/how-i-built-this-with-guy-raz/id1150510297

### 5. Masters of Scale
Best for learning about scaling companies, building teams, systems, leadership, growth, and operational thinking.

Link: https://mastersofscale.com/

### 6. The Pitch
Best for understanding how investors think, how startups pitch, and how founders answer tough funding questions.

Link: https://www.thepitch.show/

### 7. Decoder
Best for understanding CEO decision-making, technology, AI, platforms, media, digital transformation, and modern business strategy.

Link: https://www.theverge.com/decoder-podcast-with-nilay-patel

## Arabic Business Podcasts

### 1. Sawalif Business | سوالف بزنس
One of the best Arabic business podcasts for real stories from Saudi and Gulf markets, entrepreneurship, operations, and business growth.

Link: https://thmanyah.com/podcasts/swalif

YouTube Playlist: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLeNNUmE-BlnE5GVCM_aZtWCLzCzHYA-ho

### 2. Fnjan by Thmanyah | فنجان من ثمانية
Not purely business-focused, but it has strong episodes about economics, Saudi Arabia, society, leadership, companies, and market shifts.

Link: https://thmanyah.com/podcasts/fnjan
